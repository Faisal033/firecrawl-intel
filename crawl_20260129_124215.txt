Reducing  
Technology  
Friction

While technology is evolving at a supersonic pace, its adoption is delayed significantly due to many ever-evolving tangible and intangible barriers. This technology friction or the gap between the rate of technological innovation and the speed of its adoption is being actively reduced by Redington.

Redington is a thriving technology solution provider valued at over US$10 billion, with a network of 450+ international brands across 40 markets in the IT space. We are spearheading swift technology adoption across emerging global markets by leveraging our robust ecosystem, expanding partnerships and ingenious operational innovations.
